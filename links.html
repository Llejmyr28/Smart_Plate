Here are some links to reputable organizations and resources in the Philippines that provide valuable information about health, nutrition, and well-being. We encourage you to explore these resources to learn more and make informed choices.

Government Health Organizations

Department of Health (DOH) - Philippines

The official website of the Philippine Department of Health. Find information on national health programs, disease prevention, healthy lifestyle guidelines, and more.

Food and Drug Administration (FDA) - Philippines

Learn about food safety regulations, food labeling, and consumer advisories related to food products in the Philippines.

Professional Organizations

The Philippine Association of Nutrition, Inc.

The official organization for registered nutritionists and dietitians in the Philippines. Find resources related to the profession and possibly locate qualified professionals.

Educational Institutions & Research

University of the Philippines Diliman - College of Home Economics

Explore the College of Home Economics at UP Diliman for research and information related to food science and nutrition in the Philippine context.

University of Santo Tomas - Faculty of Pharmacy

UST's Faculty of Pharmacy provides research on food science, nutrition, and the medicinal properties of plants in the Philippines.
